Our Story

Welcome to Tales of Mala. I am Mariyamma Vakkachen, and I welcome you all to Mala.

Mala is a small village where our house is situated on the banks of a field. My husband Vakkachen was a planter and a nature lover. He himself planted pepper, mangosteen, nutmeg, and other exotic trees and plants in our property. Our place is famous for its serene beauty.

Since he passed away years back, I am living with my maid, and we both together cook traditional Kerala dishes. I want you all to experience the nature, food, and serenity of our place.

Hope you all will have a wonderful stay with me.
